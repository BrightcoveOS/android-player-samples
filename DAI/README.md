# DAI (Google Dynamic Ad Insertion)

These samples demonstrate server-side ad insertion with Google DAI through the Brightcove DAI plugin, where ads are stitched into the stream by Google.

## Requirements

- **Platform:** Android phone and tablet.
- **Minimum OS:** Android 5.0 (API 21).
- **Toolchain:** Android Studio, JDK 17, Gradle 8.13.
- **Extra SDKs:** the Brightcove DAI plugin, resolved from Maven and wired automatically by the build.

## Setup

The sample plays from the shared Brightcove demo account (`5420904993001`) with a Google DAI sample stream.
